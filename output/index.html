Notes on Data Science, Machine Learning, & Artificial Intelligence

I am a data scientist with a Ph.D. in quantitative political science and a decade of experience working in statistical learning, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.

Years ago I started posting my personal notes on statistics, data science, and programming. Since then the site has grown and today is visited by thousands everyday looking for information on everything from regression assumptions to distributed computing. This project is open source and available on GitHub.
